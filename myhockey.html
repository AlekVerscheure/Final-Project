Hockey

Another sport I play and greatly enjoy is Hockey. Since I am half Canadian and have a Canadian citizenship, I was born to love hockey. I believe hockey is one of the best sports out there as it is one of the hardest sports to learn how to play. You have to combine two completely different skills, skating and stick skills and combine them to play. In all other sports you grow up learning to run, catch, and even throw. With hockey you don't get to skate as often as you walk and run, so it takes a lot longer to learn. When I am out on the ice all the worries of reality disappear and it's just me and the puck.




Colorado Avalanche Hockey
Not only do I play hockey, but I will watch and go to Avalanche games frequently during the season. During the 2021-22 season the Avalanche had an outstanding season breaking multiple franchise records and hopefully getting the reward of the Stanley Cup. Getting to experience an Avs game live is just exhilarating. You feel the energy of 18,000 fans and feel the Avs success.
